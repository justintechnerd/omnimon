Project Overview

Omnimon started as a passion project to recreate the magic of classic virtual pets while bringing modern gaming sensibilities and customization options to the genre. Our goal is to provide a platform that's both nostalgic and innovative.

Key Features

  • ▸ Modular Architecture: Add new pets, features, and gameplay mechanics through modules
  • ▸ Evolution System: Complex branching evolution paths based on care and choices
  • ▸ Battle System: Engage in strategic battles with other virtual pets
  • ▸ Module Editor: Create and share custom content with the community

Technology Stack

Omnimon is built using Python and Pygame, making it accessible for modders and contributors. The modular design allows for easy extension and customization without affecting the core game systems.

Community

Join our growing community of virtual pet enthusiasts, modders, and developers. Share your modules, get help with the game, and contribute to the project's development on GitHub.
