General Settings

  • Name Format: Template string for pet folder names. '$' is replaced with the pet's name. Example: "$_dmc" creates folders like "Agumon_dmc". Used for sprite loading paths. #MODULEFILEFORMAT
  • Ruleset: Defines the type of mini-game used in the module's Adventure Mode and the way traited eggs are obtained. Also defines the bonuses for power. #MODULERULESET
  • Adventure Mode: Enables or disables adventure mode gameplay features. Required to add the module's Adventure Mode to the Battle Menu. #MODULEADVENTUREMODE
  • Condition Heart: When enabled, uses condition hearts system instead of mistake counter. Each care mistake reduces condition hearts. When disabled, mistakes are counted directly for evolution requirements and death conditions. #MODULECARECONDITIONHEART
  • Enable Shaken Egg: Allows eggs to be "shaken" (shake_counter >= 99) for evolution bonuses. It can provide a power bonus. #MODULECARESHAKENEGG
  • Back to Sleep Time: Minutes before pet automatically returns to sleep after being woken up during sleep hours. Prevents pets from staying awake when they should be sleeping. #MODULECAREBACKTOSLEEPTIME
  • Disturbance Penalty Max: Maximum penalty from sleep disturbances that affects battle sick chance. Each disturbance adds 2 to penalty. Higher values increase chance of getting sick after losing battles. #MODULECARESDISTURBANCEPENALTY

Care - Meat Settings

  • Weight Gain: Weight increase when feeding meat/hunger food. Applied only to pets above stage 1 and below 99 weight. Higher values make pets gain weight faster from eating. #MODULECAREMEATWEIGHTGAIN
  • Hunger Gain: Amount of hunger restored per meat feeding. Pets can't eat when hunger equals stomach capacity or during overfeed timer. Higher values fill hunger faster per feeding. #MODULECAREMEATHUNGERGAIN
  • Care Mistake Time: Minutes of empty hunger before triggering a care mistake. Timer resets when pet is fed. Used in death condition calculations and evolution requirements. #MODULECAREMEATCAREMISTAKETIME
  • Overfeed Timer: Minutes pet refuses food after trying to overfeed. During this time, pet will display 'nope' animation when offered food. Prevents immediate re-feeding attempts. #MODULECAREOVERFEEDTIMER
  • Can Eat Sleeping: Allows pets to eat food while in nap state. When disabled, feeding attempts during sleep hours will fail and pet shows 'nope' animation. #MODULECARECANEATSLEEPING

Care - Protein Settings

  • Weight Gain: Weight increase when feeding protein/strength food. Applied only to pets above stage 1 and below 99 weight. Works similarly to meat weight gain. #MODULECAREPROTEINWEIGHTGAIN
  • Strength Gain: Amount of strength restored per protein feeding. Also added when training. Strength depletes over time and empty strength triggers care mistakes. #MODULECAREPROTEINSTRENGTHGAIN
  • DP Gain: DP is your stamina, gained every 4th protein feeding, if current DP is below maximum energy. DP is consumed during battles and affects battle participation. #MODULECAREPROTEINDPGAIN
  • Care Mistake Time: Minutes of empty strength before triggering a care mistake. Timer resets when pet receives protein. Critical for death conditions and evolution paths. #MODULECAREPROTEINCAREMISTAKETIME
  • Overdose Max: Maximum protein overdose counter that affects battle sick chance. Each protein feeding increases overdose. Higher overdose increases sick probability after losing battles. #MODULECAREPROTEINOVERDOSEMAX
  • Protein Penalty: Protein Penalty affects battle sick chance. Determines the amount of each penalty for Protein Overdose in percent. #MODULECAREPROTEINPENALTY
  • Sleep Care Mistake Timer: Minutes before triggering a care mistake for sleep neglect. Timer resets when pet gets proper sleep. Important for maintaining good care quality. #MODULECARESLEEPCAREMISTAKE

Care - Vital Values Settings

  • Vital Value Base: Minimum value of Vital Values that a pet will gain per hour if well fed and not sick. This represents the natural recovery rate when the pet is in good health. #MODULEVITALVALUEBASE
  • Vital Value Loss: Amount of Vital Values lost per minute if hungry or sick. Higher values make pets deteriorate faster when not properly cared for. #MODULEVITALVALUELOSS

Battle Settings

  • Base Sick Chance Win: Base percentage chance of getting sick after winning a battle. Modified by protein overdose and disturbance penalties. Minimum 5%, maximum 50%. #MODULEBATTLEBASESICKCHANCEWIN
  • Base Sick Chance Lose: Base percentage chance of getting sick after losing a battle. Modified by protein overdose (+10% per overdose) and disturbance penalties. Higher risk than winning. #MODULEBATTLEBASESICKCHANCELOSE
  • Attribute Advantage: Percentage advantage when pet's attribute matches favorably against enemy (Data > Virus > Vaccine > Data). Applied as damage multiplier in battle calculations. #MODULEBATTLEATTRIBUTEADVANTAGE
  • Global Hit Points: When > 0, make the module ignore pets individual Health Points. It's also used to cap the possible damage taken. This attribute is mostly obsolete with the protocol battle system. #MODULEBATTLEGLOBALHITPOINTS
  • Sequential Rounds: Controls battle area progression mechanics. When enabled, battles progress through rounds sequentially rather than allowing random round selection. #MODULEBATTLESEQUENTIALROUNDS
  • Traited Egg Starting Level: Initial level for pets hatched from traited eggs. Higher levels provide stat bonuses including increased HP, power, and attack values. Maximum depends on stage. #MODULETRAITEDEGGLEVEL

Training Settings

  • Strength Win: Effort points gained when winning training sessions. At 16+ effort, pets gain significant power bonuses. Critical for unlocking evolution paths and combat effectiveness. #MODULETRAININGSTRENGTHGAINWIN
  • Strength Lose: Strength increase from training sessions when losing. Usually less than winning gain. Helps maintain strength levels even when training performance is poor. #MODULETRAININGSTRENGTHGAINLOSE
  • Strength Multiplier: Multiplier applied to strength gains from training based on performance. Higher performance in training yields extra strength points. #MODULETRAININGSTRENGMULTIPLIER
  • Weight Win: Weight loss when winning training. Helps manage pet weight and body condition. High weight can make a pet sick. #MODULETRAININGWEIGHTWIN
  • Weight Lose: Weight loss when losing training. Usually less than winning loss. Provides alternative weight management even when training performance is poor. #MODULETRAININGWEIGHTLOSE
  • Reverse Attack Frames: Swaps attack animation frames (ATK1↔ATK2, TRAIN1↔TRAIN2) during sprite loading. Used for visual variety or correcting animation sequences in sprite sets. #MODULEREVERSEATKFRAMES

Death Settings

  • Max Injuries: Maximum injuries before death. Injuries are gained from sickness, poop neglect, and battle losses. Represents cumulative health damage over pet's lifetime. #MODULEDEATHMAXINJURIES
  • Care Mistake: Total care mistakes before death. Includes hunger, strength, sleep, and sickness mistakes. Primary death condition for neglectful care patterns. #MODULEDEATHCAREMISTAKE
  • Sick Timer: Minutes of continuous sickness before death. Timer only counts while sick status is active. Emphasizes importance of healing sick pets promptly. #MODULEDEATHSICKTIMER
  • Hunger Timer: Minutes of empty hunger before death. Death occurs after continuous hunger neglect exceeding this threshold. Works alongside care mistake system. #MODULEDEATHHUNGERMISTAKE
  • Starvation Count: Number of hunger depletion cycles before death. Each cycle occurs when hunger reaches 0 and isn't refilled. Represents severe malnutrition effects. #MODULEDEATHSTARVATIONCOUNT
  • Strength Timer: Minutes of empty strength before death. Similar to hunger timer but for strength stat. Represents weakness from lack of protein/training. #MODULEDEATHSTRENGTHTIMER
  • Stage 4-5 Mistake: Mistakes threshold for stage 4-5 pets after evolution time expires. Only applies after normal evolution window passes. Represents adult-stage care requirements. #MODULEDEATHSTAGE45MISTAKE
  • Stage 6-7 Mistake: Mistakes threshold for stage 6+ pets after 48 hours of age. Represents ultimate-level pets requiring expert care to survive long-term. #MODULEDEATHSTAGE67MISTAKE
  • Save by B Press: Enables death prevention by B button press. When enabled, gives 1 minute frame window to save pet from death conditions. Adds player interaction element to death mechanics. #MODULEDEATHSAVEBYBPRESS
  • Save by Shake: Enables death prevention by device shaking. When enabled, gives 1 minute frame window to save pet through physical interaction. Alternative to button-based saving. #MODULEDEATHSAVEBYSHAKE
  • Death by Old Age: Days of pet's life before death from old age. When set to 0, old age death is disabled. Higher values allow pets to live longer before dying of natural causes. #MODULEDEATHOLDAGE
